Our Purpose

The right fabric is not just a material choice — it's a brand statement. We make it easier to source with intention, without compromising on quality or aesthetics.

The Fabric Connector works directly with mills, agents, and fabric producers across Europe, Asia, and beyond — curating a range that spans the full spectrum of responsible sourcing. From certified organic naturals to pioneering next-gen fibres, we speak your creative language.

About TFC

A sourcing partner that speaks design.

The Fabric Connector was built to bridge the gap between ambitious creative brands and the materials that match their values. Based in Amsterdam, we have spent years building relationships with mills, agents, and producers who care as much about how fabric is made as you do about how it looks and feels.

We don't just find fabrics. We understand collections. We know the difference between a fabric that photographs beautifully and one that actually performs. And we know that sustainability, for design-driven brands, has to come without compromise.

Years of Sourcing

What we stand for,
and how it shows.

Responsible sourcing is not a feature we bolt on — it's woven into every recommendation we make. These are the principles that guide how we curate, advise, and connect.

01 — Integrity

Radical Transparency

We tell you exactly where a fabric comes from, how it's certified, and any limitations — no greenwashing, no vague claims.

02 — Curation

Quality Over Volume

We work with a selective network of suppliers who meet our standards for craft, ethics, and material quality. We'd rather say no than recommend the wrong thing.

03 — Partnership

Long-Term Thinking

We build sourcing relationships that grow with your brand — not transactional, but consultative. Your collections become better season by season.

04 — Innovation

Material Curiosity

We actively scout new materials so you don't have to. First access to next-gen fabrics, before they reach mass adoption.

05 — Craft

Respect for Making

Behind every fabric is a mill with people, expertise, and heritage. We treat those relationships with the same care we bring to working with brands.

06 — Impact

Meaningful Reduction

Whether through deadstock, certified naturals, or next-gen alternatives, every sourcing decision we guide reduces the footprint of fashion — one metre at a time.
