Who We Are

That phrase means two things. Both are true. The Fabric Connector was originally founded by Bart and Bas Slootman — father and son. Bridging 30+ years of industry experience with a new perspective on what the future needs.

And it shapes what we do. Because the next generation of fabric sourcing isn't just about who's running the business — it's about what materials we believe fashion should be built from.

Natural fibres sourced with integrity. Next-gen materials that are genuinely production-ready. Deadstock that deserves a second life. We built The Fabric Connector to make those materials easier to access, and the decisions around them easier to make.

How We
Work.

The Fabric Connector is a curated fabric library. We scout and vet suppliers, building a selection of natural, next-gen, and deadstock materials that meet our standards for quality, provenance, and integrity.

Brands can explore the library remotely, or visit us in person at our Amsterdam showroom. Samples are sent out on request — because the right fabric decision starts with holding it in your hands.

01 — The Library

A Library You Can Touch

Browse remotely or visit our Amsterdam showroom in person. Natural fibres, next-gen materials, and deadstock — all vetted, documented, and ready to request samples from.

02 — The Sourcing

Working Alongside Your Team

For brands that want more than access to materials — we consult directly alongside design teams, helping evaluate options, understand certifications, and build a material direction that fits both creative vision and production reality.

03 — The Network

200+ Mill Partners

Not everything in our network is listed in the library — especially deadstock, which moves fast. Leave a request and we'll come back with options sourced directly from our supplier relationships.

What We
Stand For.

These are the principles that guide how we curate, advise, and connect. Not a mission statement — just how we work.

01

Radical Transparency

We tell you exactly where a fabric comes from, how it's certified, and any limitations.

02

Quality Over Volume

A selective supplier network. Not a directory — a curated list. Every mill meets our standards for craft, ethics, and material quality.

03

Long-Term Thinking

We build sourcing relationships that grow with your brand. Consultative, not transactional.

04

Material Curiosity

We actively scout next-gen materials and emerging fibres. First access before the mainstream catches up.

05

Respect for Making

Behind every fabric is a mill with people, expertise, and heritage. We treat those relationships accordingly.
